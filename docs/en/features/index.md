# Project Introduction

NodeAuth is a **high-security**, **lightweight**, and **cross-platform** 2FA/TOTP management tool.

Whether you are pursuing the extreme cost-performance of **Serverless deployment** or the absolute data control of **native private deployment**, NodeAuth perfectly adapts to your needs, providing a smooth 2FA secret management experience like a native App.

---

## 💎 Design Philosophy: The Dual Pursuit

NodeAuth is more than just a tool; it is the product of our relentless pursuit of **Ulitmate Security** and **Ultimate Experience**.

### 🛡️ Ultimate Security
**"The Unshakable Data Foundation"**
*   **End-to-End Encryption**: Core data is protected with **WebAuthn hardware salt** injection and AES-GCM high-strength cold encryption.
*   **Sovereign Ownership**: Whether using D1 or MySQL, your data always stays locked in the storage you specify.
*   **Active Defense**: Built-in environment sniffing, anti-snoop blurring, and self-healing synchronization mechanisms protect your identity assets 24/7.
> [➔ Explore Security Details](./security)

### ⚡ Ultimate Experience
**"The Art of Seamless Interaction"**
*   **Native-level PWA**: Install to your desktop or home screen with one click. Supports completely offline operation, so your dynamic codes never "lose power".
*   **Seamless Migration**: Supports one-stop migration from 20+ mainstream apps like Bitwarden, GA, 1Password, etc.
*   **Micro-motion Design**: Visual chunking, Next Code pre-reading, and smooth transition animations make every unlock graceful.
> [➔ Explore Experience Details](./experience)

## 💡 Why Choose NodeAuth?

We believe security should not come at the cost of convenience. Through an advanced **WASM core** and **offline-first architecture**, NodeAuth provides an experience comparable to top-tier commercial software even in extreme environments where privacy is paramount.
