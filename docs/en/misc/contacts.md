# Contact & Roadmap

The growth of NodeAuth is inseparable from the feedback and support of our community users.

---

## 📩 Contact Us
If you encounter problems, find bugs, or have suggestions for functional improvements during use:
*   **GitHub Issues**: [NodeAuth Repository](https://github.com/nodeauth/nodeauth-worker) (Recommended).
*   **Telegram Discussion Group**: [NodeAuth Group](https://t.me/NodeauthGroup).
*   **Emergency Recovery Pack**: Please refer to the [Disaster Recovery Guide](../recovery/index.md) to learn how to use the PDF file containing your encryption keys for account recovery.

## 🗺️ Roadmap
We plan to add the following features in future versions:

- [ ] **Develop Native App**: Based on Tauri/Rust to provide ultimate performance and system integration experience for desktop and mobile.
- [ ] **More OAuth Providers**: Add support for GitLab, Bitbucket, Slack, etc.
- [ ] **Language Expansion**: Add support for Japanese, Russian, etc.

## 🤝 Contributing
In the spirit of 100% open source, NodeAuth invites developers to join our ranks. According to the **Roadmap**, we currently urgently need expert support in the following areas:

1.  **Native App Development (Tauri / Rust)**:
    *   **Requirements**: Familiar with the Rust language and the Tauri framework.
    *   **Goal**: Migrate PWA functionality to a cross-platform native shell, integrating deeper system notification and biometric APIs.
2.  **Multilingual Community Support (i18n)**:
    *   **Requirements**: Native speakers or professional translators in Japanese, Russian, German, etc.
    *   **Goal**: Review and correct professional vocabulary deviations in automatic translations.

If you are interested in participating, please contact us directly in the [Telegram Discussion Group](https://t.me/NodeauthGroup).
