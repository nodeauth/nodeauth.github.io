# PWA Installation Guide

NodeAuth is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. Once installed, it appears on your phone's home screen or your computer's taskbar just like a native app, providing a better full-screen experience and superior offline capabilities.

---

## 📱 Installation on Mobile (iOS / Android)

### 1. iOS (iPhone / iPad)
Due to Apple's system restrictions, please ensure you are using the **Safari browser**:
1. Tap the "**Share**" icon (a square with an up arrow) in the center of the bottom taskbar.
2. Scroll down in the menu that appears and tap "**Add to Home Screen**".
3. Tap "Add" in the top right corner. NodeAuth will now appear on your home screen.

### 2. Android
We recommend using the **Chrome browser** or your device's built-in browser:
1. Tap the "three dots" menu in the top right corner of the browser.
2. Find and tap "**Install App**" or "**Add to Home Screen**".
3. Once confirmed, the app icon will appear on your home screen or in your app drawer.

---

## 💻 Installation on Desktop (Windows / macOS / Linux)

We recommend using **Chrome** or **Edge** browsers:
1. On the right side of the address bar, you will see a small "**Install**" icon (a monitor with an arrow).
2. Click that icon and select "**Install**".
3. NodeAuth will now run as a standalone window. You can pin it to your taskbar or Dock.

---

## ✨ Benefits of Installation
*   **Immersive Experience**: Hides the browser's address bar and toolbars, leaving every inch of screen space for your data.
*   **Instant Offline Startup**: Achieves "zero-white-screen" startup through local caching.
